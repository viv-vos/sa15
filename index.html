Recipe One

Simple Chocolate Cake

Description:

Light, fluffy, airy. Topped with creamy, thick cream cheese frosting. This chocolate cake will bring back nostalgia of birthday cakes from your childhood. It is easy simple and only has a few ingredients to whip up a quick and easy cake! Bring out your creative side in different baking pans and dye your frosting different colors to enjoy this cake for any celebration!!!

Ingredients:

Estimated Time: 1 hour and 30 minutes

Chocolate Cake

  • Boxed chocolate cake
  • 2 sticks of butter
  • ¾ cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 3 large eggs

Cream Cheese Frosting

  • 4 oz of Cream Cheese, at room temperature
  • 2 sticks of butter
  • 2 Tbsp of Unsalted Butter, at room temperature
  • ½ tsp of Vanilla Extract
  • 1 to 2 Tbsp of Milk
  • 2 cups of Confectioner Sugar

Steps:

    Cake:

  1. Mix all ingredients together and bake as instructed

    Cream Cheese Frosting:

  1. In a large bowl cream together the butter, cream cheese and vanilla, add the confectioner sugar and continue to whisk until it’s all incorporated.
  2. Add the milk and whisk until everything is combined. Cover with plastic wrap for 1 hour before using.
  3. Use this frosting to top any cupcake, cake or cookie.

Recipe Two

Chewy Brownie Recipe

Description:

Chocolately, chewy, and delicious brownies. These are perfect for any occasion! Even if it is just a night in with your favorite movie, these brownies will be a hit!!!

Ingredients:

Estimated Time: 55 minutes

  • 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2/3 cup cocoa powder, sifted if lumpy
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar, sifted if lumpy
  • 1/2 cup dark chocolate chips
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla
  • 3/4 teaspoons sea salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • 1/2 cup canola oil or extra-virgin olive oil

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 325°F. Lightly spray an 8x8 baking dish (not a 9x9 dish or your brownies will overcook) with cooking spray and line it with parchment paper. Spray the parchment paper.
  2. In a medium bowl, combine the sugar, flour, cocoa powder, powdered sugar, chocolate chips, and salt.
  3. In a large bowl, whisk together the eggs, olive oil, water, and vanilla.
  4. Sprinkle the dry mix over the wet mix and stir until just combined.
  5. Pour the batter into the prepared pan (it'll be thick - that's ok) and use a spatula to smooth the top. Bake for 40 to 48 minutes, or until a toothpick comes out with only a few crumbs attached (note: it's better to pull the brownies out early than to leave them in too long). Cool completely before slicing.*** Store in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days. These also freeze well!

Recipe Three

Chocolate Pudding

Description:

Simple, chill, and refreshing treat for the summer days coming up ahead!

Ingredients:

Estimated Time: 2 hours 10 minutes

  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/3 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 3 Tbsp cornstarch
  • 2 cups half and half
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Steps:

  1. Add the sugar, cocoa powder, and cornstarch to a small sauce pot and stir to combine.
  2. Add the half and half and vanilla extract to the sauce pot. Place the pot over medium heat and begin to whisk everything together. The cocoa will repel the liquid at first, but as the half and half begins to warm, they will come together.
  3. Continue to heat the mixture while whisking until it comes to a gentle simmer, at which point it will thicken into a pudding. Remove the pudding from the heat.
  4. Serve the chocolate pudding warm, or refrigerate until cool. To refrigerate, place a layer or plastic wrap or parchment on the surface of the pudding to prevent a skin from forming on top.
